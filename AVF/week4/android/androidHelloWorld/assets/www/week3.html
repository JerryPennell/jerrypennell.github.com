Mobile Apps: Please list two apps (either Android or iOS, or both) that are made specifically to support Accessibility.

Aesop for Children is an interactive story application made for screen readers on both platforms. EyeNote is a mobile device application to denominate Federal Reserve Notes (U.S. paper currency) as an aid for the blind or visually impaired to increase accessibility.
